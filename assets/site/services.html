Photo management and storage

Immich is a polished photo and video management system. It is designed for uploading, storing, and sharing media with good support for sharing as well as bells and whistles like face recognition and geotagging.

There are three ways you can use Immich:

Web
Visit $IMMICH_SUBDOMAIN.■ and sign in.
iOS
Download the Immich app from the App Store, then supply the server URL https://$IMMICH_URL. Log in.
Android
Download the Immich app from the Play Store (or F-Droid), then supply the server URL https://$IMMICH_URL. Log in.

When using the iOS or Android app, after logging in you can select albums on the device to back up (tap on the cloud icon next to the top right corner). I recommend Camera on Android and Recent on iOS.

Cloud storage

You can easily access the storage from the web at $SFTPGO_SUBDOMAIN.■. The first time you log in, your account will automatically be created.
