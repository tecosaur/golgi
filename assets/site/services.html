With a little effort, it's not too hard to migrate from backup drives or cloud storage, thanks to the tool immich-go. If you're happy running a simple command line tool, you can download the appropriate binary for your system, and figure out how to use it.

Document management

Paperless is a document management system, designed for scanning, storing, and tagging documents such as receipts, bills, letters, and manuals.

Because paperwork and filing is no fun, it learns from the labels and categories you assign to documents, and will automatically apply them to new documents. Simply throw everything in, and rely on the tagging and full-text search to dig out documents with ease later.

You can use Paperless a few different ways:

Web
Visit $PAPERLESS_SUBDOMAIN.‚ñ† and sign in.
iOS
You may download the 3rd party Swift Paperless from the App Store. QuickScan also has built-in support for Paperless.
Android
There's Paperless Mobile on the Play Store (or F-Droid). It hasn't been updated in a while though.
